Useful Product Information
In order to reorganize product details and lists, the most important issue was to think about how to clearly convey various information. To this end, the importance of information was classified when designing a product list and detailed product, and designed based on the information hierarchy of the order of product price, brand, label, and review.

New Visual Language, Bridged Line
The new visual language of the Seoul Store was designed with inspiration from the spatial characteristics of Seoul that connects Gangnam and Gangbuk and the characteristics of the Seoul Store service that connects influencers, brands, and customers. A graphic motif was obtained from the most representative object of'bridge' (leg) that connects space and relations, and a concept was designed using two straight lines. We designed a new visual language.
Grid System
The'Bridged Line' for a new visual language is the basis and standard of all designs in the Seoul Store. The grid system that can be utilized based on the'Bridged Line' has been newly designed. Grid provides a standard to express and utilize various visual elements such as titles, images (content), and subtitles. In addition, the grid can be flexibly transformed according to the designer used.
BX 2.0 Web Page & Application
The web page was designed using thick lines as graphic motifs in'Bridged Line', and since it contains a lot of text and images, it was designed to feel the difference in visual hierarchy based on the gap between typography and content. When designing online and offline applications, the design was based on the'Bridged Line' motif and grid. In particular, the offline application was designed with a focus on designing the'Bridged Line' itself to be used as the identity of a friend's discount code.

Brand of My goods service
My goods service is the second service of D Unit and is operated in conjunction with the Seoul Store. To deliver the same brand experience as the Seoul Store, logos and applications were created using the brand elements of the Seoul Store BX 2.0 Guide.
User Interface Structure
My Goods is designed to be divided into 3 parts LNB, search & menu bar at the top, and content area for convenient usability. Users can move through the LNB and menu bar located at any depth and page, and the accessibility has been increased to make goods easier.
Tools For Customization
The custom tool is one of the parts that I paid the most attention to when designing My Goods. Intuitive icons and text guides were used so that general users and creators would have no difficulty in using them, and we focused on providing consistent visuals even if the functions of each custom tool are completely different.
Make Easy & Custom Easy
In addition to custom tools, the process of designing the flow of making goods directly was very challenging. The screen was divided into 3 parts, showing the guide on the production stage on the left, the view area where you can directly see the goods being made in the middle, and the custom tools required for production on the right. In addition, for convenient expression during production, various resources such as fonts, images, and stickers are provided in advance to enhance usability.
Mygoods Store
The My Goods Store is an essential element of the My Goods project. We designed the profile image and store main image to be customizable for creators' free expression, and designed affordance to the follow button so that creators and fans can maintain a relationship with each other.
Settlement System
Creators can receive the sales amount excluding commissions based on the sales history of goods. The settlement system must expose various information such as the amount that can be settled, the number of sales, and the total amount of settlement that occur during settlement. When designing, we focused on expressing this information clearly.

Overview
The Seoul Store is a fashion commerce service for the 1020 generations who are sensitive to trends, and it is a service that allows you to meet the contents and brand products of various collaborative creators. Seoul Store is based on the'Friend Discount Code', where not only creators but also general users can earn money by showing and sharing their style, My Store, Seoul Gram, and'Preorder', where you can see popular fashion products in advance. Operating and showing.
Over a long period of time, services, projects, and functions that need to be opened have increased, which is inconsistent for users. It came to provide an uncomfortable experience. To solve this, we tried to solve it based on a clear UX design and a UI design with principles.
UX structure
3 Dept User Flow
The Seoul Store is basically designed based on 3 Dept, including menus, detail pages, and actual action pages such as payment. The menu and the detail page are organically connected, and users can always quickly access the action page to achieve what they want.
Useful Product Information
In order to reorganize product details and lists, the most important issue was to think about how to clearly convey various information. To this end, the importance of information was classified when designing a product list and detailed product, and designed based on the information hierarchy of the order of product price, brand, label, and review.
Easy To Access, Easy To Make
The amount of issuance of friend discount codes that exploded at the beginning of the opening decreased significantly over time, As new services opened, we identified the problem with the conclusion that the accessibility of the friend's discount code was inferior. In order to reactivate the friend discount code, which is the main service, we boldly conducted a test to expose it in the form of a floating button at the bottom of the home and the top of My Page.
Promotion Template
Based on the fact that users show high click-through rates to famous brands, we designed a structure in which major brands are placed at the top, single at the bottom, and then themes.
Promotion Image
As the promotion structure was reorganized, the image communication method was also newly improved. In order to increase the visibility of the brand, the brand logo was directly exposed, and the theme and product were also improved with a card image to effectively convey information.
